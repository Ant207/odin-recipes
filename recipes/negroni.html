Negroni Cocktail Recipe

Description

A classic Italian Cocktail consisting of equal parts gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz Gin "I prefer Tanquaray No.10"
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
  • Orange peel garnish

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir until well chilled.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube.
  4. Garnish with an orange peel twist.
