Margarita Cocktail Recipe

Description

A classic Mexican cocktail made with tequila, triple sec, and lime juice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz Triple Sec
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • Salt (for rimming the glass)

Steps:

  1. Rim a glass with salt.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a shaker filled with ice.
  3. Shake well until chilled.
  4. Strain into the prepared glass over fresh ice.
